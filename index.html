About me

I love Software Engineering and Computer Networking, and so I have a PhD in Information and Communications Technology from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (Spain). My main interests are Web Engineering, Open Source, Software Testing, Multimedia and Java.

I work as a Software Engineer/Researcher at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (Spain). We are developing Kurento, an open source framework devoted to simplify the development of rich multimedia applications.

I also work as an Assistant Professor at U-tad, Centro Universitario de Tecnología y Arte Digital (Spain). I teach "Introduction to Computer Networks" and "Web Programming".
